Arjun Mehta

Founder & Head Chef

A Passion Born in the Kitchen

"Eating healthy is not a punishment — it's the most beautiful gift you can give to your body and soul."

Arjun Mehta grew up in the culturally rich city of Ranchi, where food was never just sustenance — it was a language of love. After training at culinary institutes across India, he returned home with one mission: to serve food that nourishes the body and uplifts the spirit.

At THE SAMPLE ONE, we believe that every ingredient tells a story. We source locally, cook mindfully, and serve with warmth. Our menu is a tribute to the diverse flavors of Jharkhand, reimagined for the modern palate.

"A restaurant is not just a place to eat — it's a place to feel at home."

We are committed to zero artificial preservatives, sustainable sourcing, and creating a dining experience that leaves you healthier and happier than when you arrived.
